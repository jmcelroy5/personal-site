Reading

A book is my happy place. This is a list of the books I've read in recent years. It's for myself to be able to look back on them and for viewers to maybe discover something they want to read. I also track my reading on Goodreads and occasionally feel moved to write a review there. Maintaining a digital bookshelf has made it easier for me to let go of physical books and share them more freely with friends or little libraries in the neighborhood.
